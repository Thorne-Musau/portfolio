Projects

Medical Insurance Cover Prediction

Apr 2024 - Aug 2024

The project aims at predicting the likelihood that someone has purchased or is in possession of a Medical insurance cover based on various Demographic and Social-political factors.

The prediction is based on the identification of key features that mainly contribute significantly to the assessment of one being in possession of a Medical Insurance cover. This project involved:

  • Data collection and preprocessing of demographic and social-political data
  • Feature engineering and selection to identify the most relevant factors
  • Development and comparison of multiple machine learning models
  • Model evaluation and fine-tuning for optimal performance
  • Creation of a user-friendly interface for inputting data and receiving predictions

The final model achieved an accuracy of 85% in predicting medical insurance cover possession, providing valuable insights for insurance companies and policymakers.
